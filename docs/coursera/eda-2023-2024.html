Key Findings and Strategic Insights

Single Session Program Model Validation

  • Continuous Learning: 136 learners successfully enrolled in single session design
  • Extended Timeline: Learners active across both 2023 and 2024 completion years
  • Flexible Progression: Some learners joined in 2023 but were most active in 2024

Course Completion Year Analysis

  • 2023 Completion Focus: 90 learners with primary activity in 2023
  • 2024 Completion Focus: 45 learners with primary activity in 2024
  • Extended Engagement: Demonstrates value of extended timeline for working adults

Application Patterns

  • Primary Launch: 315 applications in 2023 (98.4% of total)
  • Extended Access: 4 applications in 2024 (limited late interest)
  • Strong Conversion: 43.2% overall application-to-enrollment rate

Strategic Recommendations

  1. Maintain Single Session Design: Data validates continuous program model over annual cohorts
  2. Plan Extended Timeline: 18-24 month duration appropriate for course completion patterns
  3. Focus Launch Efforts: Concentrate marketing in Q3 for maximum application response
  4. Support Extended Learning: Provide ongoing support for learners completing across multiple years
  5. Track Completion Years: Use latest activity date to understand when learners are most engaged

Performance Benchmarks

  • Target Application Volume: 200+ annually (based on 2023 success)
  • Target Conversion Rate: 40%+ (maintain strong performance)
  • Target Completion Distribution: Balance between first-year and extended-year completions
  • Session Duration: 18-24 months for optimal completion rates
